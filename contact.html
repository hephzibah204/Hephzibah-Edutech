Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer online training? ▼
Yes! We offer flexible online training options including live virtual classes and self-paced modules accessible from anywhere in Nigeria.
How long does each training program take? ▼
Program duration varies from 1 week (bootcamp) to 3 months (full course). We also offer customized in-school programs that fit your school term calendar.
Can you come to our school to train students? ▼
Absolutely. We offer in-school training delivery across Ogun State and neighboring regions. Contact us to schedule a visit and customize a program for your school.
Do students receive a certificate? ▼
Yes. All participants who successfully complete any of our training programs receive an official Hephzibah Edutech certificate that can be added to their portfolio and CV.
Do you build websites for schools? ▼
Yes. We design and develop professional school websites, school management systems, CBT platforms, result management systems, and custom web applications for educational institutions.
How do I book a consultation? ▼
Simply fill out the contact form on this page, call +234 907 778 0156, or email hephzibahedutech@gmail.com. We'll schedule a free initial consultation at your convenience.
